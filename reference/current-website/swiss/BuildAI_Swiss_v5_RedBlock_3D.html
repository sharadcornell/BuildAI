01 — The Goal

Close the gap between
graduating and shipping.

Colleges teach theory. Startups hire shipping. BuildAI closes that gap in 13 weeks — with deployed products as the only proof.

  • NOTAn AI course — students build, they don't watch
  • NOTA bootcamp — it runs alongside the semester
  • NOTA certificate mill — ~30% certify, by design
  • ISAn apprenticeship with working AI-startup engineers

02 — The Programme
