§1

Design Intent — The Goal

Colleges produce theory. AI-first startups hire shipping velocity, engineering judgment and AI-tool fluency. This apprenticeship closes that gap in 13 weeks — with deployed products as the only acceptable proof.

NOTAn AI course — students build; nobody watches videos
NOTA bootcamp — runs alongside the semester, no career break
NOTA certificate mill — ~30% certify, by design
ISAn apprenticeship reviewed weekly by working AI-startup engineers
§2
