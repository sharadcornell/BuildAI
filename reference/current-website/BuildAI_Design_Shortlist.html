The Program · Section 04

A thirteen-week apprenticeship for engineers who'd rather ship than sit through slides.

A new kind of programme is being built for India's tier-2 engineering colleges — one that treats undergraduates the way an AI startup treats its first three hires.
Words — BuildAI / Photographs — Demo Day archive
For every hundred undergraduates in a tier-2 CS programme, perhaps twenty have the instincts of a builder. They write code that wasn't assigned. They deploy on weekends. They watch what their seniors did at startups and want that, not the campus placement queue. BuildAI exists for them — and for the colleges that quietly already know who they are.
