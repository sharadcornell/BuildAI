01The Gap

Engineering curriculum ends where
AI-native engineering begins.

Indian colleges produce strong theoretical foundations. AI-first startups hire for something else entirely: shipping velocity, engineering judgment, and fluency with AI-assisted workflows. That gap is structural — and closeable in 13 weeks if the apprenticeship is rigorous.

Where curriculum ends

  • Limited time for shipping practice
  • Production deployment rarely taught
  • AI tooling changes every quarter
  • Startup workflows hard to simulate

What startups need

  • AI-assisted development fluency
  • Product & architecture judgment
  • Evaluation discipline for AI features
  • Async collaboration habits
